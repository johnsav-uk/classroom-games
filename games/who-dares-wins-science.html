How to play (host notes)

Each round a science category is announced. Teams alternate turns, either raising the bid (claiming they can name one more correct item than the current bid) or daring their opponents to play at the standing bid. Whoever is dared must then name that many correct, non-repeated answers in a row — succeed and they win the list; slip up once and the other team takes it.

First team to win 2 lists becomes champion and moves on to the Money Round, naming as many answers as possible from a fresh category (up to 15), banking winnings every 3 correct — but risking it all if they push their luck too far. A 1–1 split is settled with a sudden-death tiebreaker list.

As each team calls out an answer, you (the host) type it into the box. Correct answers climb the ladder from the bottom up; one wrong, repeated, or stuck answer flashes the ladder red and ends the round instantly. You can peek at the hidden answer key on your own screen if you need to check a spelling — just keep it out of view of the teams.
